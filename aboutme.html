ARTIST STATEMENT

In the realm of my lens, every shadow, every line, and every form tells a story. Sadly you will never fully understand the emotions I hold but you can glimpse and interpret what my lens will tell you. ‘The world I leave behind will be no different than how I entered’. My hope in life is to change this line by some significance, to change the world for the better. To bring meaning to something unalive like my photos is extraordinary, but to bring meaning to the lives who view them is absolute. My project will focus on how all of us in our lives are able to change based on our significance in environment. If you can change your place you can change your face.
